# Brian "Magus" Nelson (he/him)
Magus is a minor character in the story but an interesting one. He's Emily's and Kai's partner and somewhat removed from the situation. He's also technically minded and has access to some magical talents, ones he hasn't used in a long time. 

He's 43 years old, has multiple sclerosis (though it's mild in his case), and loves video games!
