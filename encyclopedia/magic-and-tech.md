# Interactions Between Magic and Technology
Where magic and technology collide. In this world, magic is real but diminished — rare, expensive, and often treated with caution or suspicion — which makes it a tool of the few rather than the many.

## Tech-neutralizing wards
Some magic users can create wards that neutralize most technology within a limited space, creating small, controlled areas where technology simply doesn't function. This enables meetings held entirely free of technology — no drones, no recording, no connected devices, no surveillance. It is not cheap. In practice, this puts it within reach mainly of the ultra-wealthy, who use it to their advantage — private conversations that can't be listened in on are a luxury good.
