# One Body One Soul (OBOS)
## Overview
OBOS is a human-purity movement aimed at preserving the human species and pushing back anything that takes away from that humanity. In addition to advocating against augmentations and the proliferation of AI, they present themselves as protectors of what it means to be human. Privately, they sponsor and encourage offshoot organizations that deploy violence in the name of their cause.

The organization's leaders claim that their stance is wholly secular and grounded in science and logic, but they align themselves with religious and spiritual organizations, suggesting that they're not picky about who they work with.

The public side of OBOS holds rallies, files briefs, buys ad time, and presents itself as non-violent protectors of humanity. The terrorist organizations they quietly support have been known to bomb augmentation clinics and data centers. 

Various counterterrorism organizations have OBOS on their radar, and Keith has been dispatched at least twice to deal with a group affiliated with OBOS. Thus far, the organization itself has not been held responsible.

Recently, OBOS took a very public stand in a trial regarding the personhood of a supposedly sapient AI, against the AI in question. This earned them some legitimacy and engagement, along with donations to their cause.

## Doctorine
Their doctrine is in the name. Each human has one body and one mind or soul, and everything else merely taints that already perfect design. They argue against eugenics and augmentation, viewing both in the same light, and consider AI to be either fraud or abomination.

- Sapient AI are seen as lacking a body and a soul, so they view legal personhood of such entities as gibberish.
- OBOS has a disdain for shapeshifters based on the idea that no human should ever become an animal and that people with such talents should be barred from using them.
- They believe that companies like Sincline are dooming humanity to a slow decline and decry augmentations as a transhumanist scam meant to enslave everyone.
- Notably, OBOS doesn't despise all magic simply because it exists. In their eyes, magic is a natural part of the world but should be used cautiously and only in line with human purity.
